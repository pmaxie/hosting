A Little About Me and My Journey in Interaction Deisgn

My name is Paris Maxie, born and raised in Atlanta, Georgia. I'm currently an Interaction Design major at Kennesaw State University and I am set to graduate Spring 2023. I received my first Bachelor's Degree in marketing at the University of West Georgia July 2019. I am interested in becoming an Interaction Designer to especially help those with disabilities experience websites and apps the same way an abled person has the opportunity to. But I am also interested in helping to create a better relationship between humans and technology. The way I see it, life is already hard enough and technology is becoming more and more essential to our every day lives. So why not be the one behind making everyone's life at least a little bit easier when using it?
